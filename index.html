Course Overview

Target Audience

Individuals seeking to enter the field of IT auditing or enhance their existing skills.

Prerequisites

Basic understanding of IT systems and general business processes.

Course Duration

Approximately 12-15 hours, including video content, exercises, and assessments.

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to IT Auditing

Learn the fundamentals of IT auditing, including its role and importance within an organization.

Module 2: Core Concepts in IT Auditing

Dive into Information Technology General Controls (ITGCs) and other core concepts.

Module 3: Auditing Frameworks and Standards

Explore the Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX), COBIT, ISO 27001, and more.

Module 4: The IT Auditing Process

Master the IT auditing process, from planning to reporting and follow-up.

Module 5: Practical Skills and Tools

Learn about the tools and techniques used in modern IT auditing.
